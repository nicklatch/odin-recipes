Mini Buscuit Pizzas

Ingredients

  • One 7.5 oz can of biscuits
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • .75 cup reduced fat mozzarella cheese
  • .5 green pepper (diced)
  • .5 medium onion (diced)
  • 1 oz turkey pepperoni
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. On a smooth service roll out each biscuit into a circle.
  3. Lay on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper. Lightly spray the dough circles with cooking spray.
  4. Add one heaping tablespoon of marinara, followed by 1 heaping tablespoon of cheese, and then evenly top with diced turkey pepperoni, green peppers, and onions.
  5. Place into preheated oven for 10-12 minutes until the cheese is melted and the biscuits are golden.
